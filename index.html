Yor'e Bazaar

YoreBazaar is a shopping site that aims to provide a more personalized shopping experience to its users. A registered user on the site can create multiple profiles for each member of their family or friends. Each individual profile has an associated cart and they have the option to add items to the cart, delete items from the cart or update item quantity. When they are ready to checkout, an order is created with the items in the cart and the cart is emptied of its contents. The users can view the orders placed for each profile thus tracking individual orders and total expense for each member. The application is built with MySQL, Node, Express, Handlebars and Sequelize and follows the MVC design pattern; uses Node and Sequelize to query and route data in the app, and Handlebars to generate the HTML.
